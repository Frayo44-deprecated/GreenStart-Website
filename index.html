About Us

We believe that traditional schools do not prepare and inspire the next generation.
These are just some of the issues in current school systems: Learning out of obligation instead of interest,
Studying long hours which burden and restrict personal life as well as personal growth,
Mainly frontal instruction which restricts creativity and analytical thinking,
A majority of knowledge taught does not prepare for the “real world” (for example modern workforce and academy preparation)

After we understood that there is a real problem with school systems today, we decided to found GreenStart. Green Start is a place where teenagers from variety of knowledge background can come, team together and bring their ideas and dreams to the real world. Our mentors and us help groups with their ideas and teach the process of creating an idea from our experience. In Green Start we believe that failures are the way for success. Currently there are 80 people in Green Start, and they all are passionate about innovation and entrepreneurship.
Our mentors and the teenagers themselves in Green Start inspire each other, they help others and transform their knowledge and skills to others.

Our Vision

In the future we want to spread to other schools. We believe that more teenagers from variety of backgrounds and ages should get these opportunities we give in Green Start. We want that every person in Green Start will finish the year with the confident that he or she knows to develop any idea that will come up to any person in the future. We always search for mentors and inspiring people with a lot of experience which will teach teenagers from their own experience. We believe that school systems are not fitted to the 21th century, and Green Start is the way to change it.
