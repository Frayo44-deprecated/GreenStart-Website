About Us


We believe that traditional schools do not prepare and inspire the next generation.
These are just some of the issues in current school systems: Learning out of obligation instead of interest,
Studying long hours which burden and restrict personal life as well as personal growth,
Mainly frontal instruction which restricts creativity and analytical thinking,
A majority of knowledge taught does not prepare for the “real world” (for example modern workforce and academy preparation)

We believe that this education system must be changed. Green Start changes the way education systems work today.
Green Start is a place where teenagers from variety of knowledge background can come, we help them bring their ideas to life.
In Green Start we believe that failures are the way for a long-way success.
